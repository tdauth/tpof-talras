Ice Elemental

By Burning_Dragoon5



Description:
Made for the Modeling Competition #10.

Basically, it's an infernal-looking-like Elemental, made of ice blocks.

It has team color (even if it's quite hard to see, glowing eyes and runes on its back) and many animations:
4 stands, 1 stand ready
2 attacks, 1 attack slam
1 spell, 1 spell slam
1 birth, 1 death
1 walk

Update: Added sounds:
- upon death
- while walking (+ footprints)
- upon birth
- upon spell slam
